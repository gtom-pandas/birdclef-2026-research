## Project Card

**BirdCLEF 2026 - Bioacoustic AI Research**

I worked on multi-label bird and wildlife soundscape classification under Kaggle's offline inference constraints. The final system combined Perch v2 audio embeddings, 5-second temporal windows, site/hour/taxonomy priors, MLP probes, ProtoSSM and ResidualSSM sequence models, EfficientNet SED experiments, and gated rank blending. The project became a strong practical study in leaderboard-driven ML, hidden-test robustness, dependency management, and fast experimental iteration.
